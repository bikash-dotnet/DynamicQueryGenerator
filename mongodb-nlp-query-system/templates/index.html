Help & Tips
What you can ask:
  • "Show me users older than 30"
  • "Find products under $100"
  • "List orders from last week"
  • "Count active users by country"
What you cannot do:
  • Delete, update, or insert data
  • Drop collections
  • Modify database structure
Tips:
  • Be specific about fields and values
  • Use natural language - no special syntax needed
  • Export results to CSV/Excel when you have more than 1 record
